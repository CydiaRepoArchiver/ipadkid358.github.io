How to jailbreak (iOS 10 - 10.2)

Video Tutorial

The following must be done on a computer with iTunes:

Visit these links and download the appropriate versions. For Yalu102, click on "ipa (cydia impactor)"

  • Yalu102
  • Cydia Impactor

Open Cydia Impactor (should have just downloaded this)

Plug the device you wish to jailbreak into the computer and make sure it appears in iTunes (check below for device compatibility)

Locate the file that you downloaded from Yalu102. You likely can drag it straight from the downloads section of your browser.

Drag the file onto Cydia Impactor, where your device name should be

Type in your Apple ID as instructed. The information is sent directly to Apple. If you're uncomfortable with this and you'd like to use one of my accounts, let me know

Yalu102 should now appear on your device. Head over to Settings → General → Profiles and Device Management and click on your email. Then click trust

Open Yalu102, and click Go! Your device will appear to crash. This is completely normal. Wait until you see your passcode screen. Unlock your device, and Cydia should be there. If it's not, try again
